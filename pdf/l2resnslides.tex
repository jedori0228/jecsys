Analysis:
\begin{itemize}
\item Data stored in TH3D ($p_{T,ave}$, $|\eta|$, $MPF$ or $p_{T,bal}$) for each of $\alpha<0.10, 0.15, 0.20, 0.30$
\item Projected and sliced from there for number of events, cross section, RMS, mean response
%\item $\sigma(asymmetry)$ is 0.985-truncated RMS (switchable from full RMS)
%\item Correction for JER bias calculated analytically based on JME Spring16 MC truth JER and SF (but not yet applied to results)
\item L2res with log-linear fit (cross-check quadratic logarithmic) versus $p_{T,ave}$
%\item {\bf known issue 1}: only raw PFMET in SMP-J root files, need raw PFchsMET
%\item {\bf known issue 2}: only jets with pT>25 GeV in SMP-J tuples, need down to 5-15 GeV for type-I MET and proper $\alpha$ cuts
%\item {\bf caveat}: using DB for central results, but not applying FSR+ISR correction; instead starting with $\alpha<max(0.1,25/p_{T,ave})$
%\item {\bf to check}: do we oversmear MC for JER?
%\item {\bf novelty:} this analysis complements $p_{T,ave}$ binning with $p_{T,probe}$ and $p_{T,tag}$ binning; these have different (anti-correlated) sensitivity to ISR+FSR and JER (in particular including non-Gaussian tails) such that the spread between these two and central $p_{T,ave}$ result is a good proxy of systematic uncertainties. Comparing MPF and DB can further factor out ISR+FSR and JER tail effects.
\item {\bf novelty:} this analysis complements $p_{T,ave}$ binning with $p_{T,tag}$ binning; this has different (anti-correlated) sensitivity to ISR+FSR and JER (in particular including non-Gaussian tails) such that the spread between this and the central $p_{T,ave}$ result is a good proxy of systematic uncertainties. Comparing MPF and DB can further factor out ISR+FSR and JER tail effects.
\item Hannu's phi cleaning applied
\end{itemize}
